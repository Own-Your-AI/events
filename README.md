## Why are we doing this?

We believe AI should be **owned, not rented**.

- **Sovereignty** over your models, your data, and your costs
- **Independence** from a handful of hyperscalers shaping the future of European engineering
- **Mastery** of the stack — from token economics to hardware to agentic workflows
- **Community** of practitioners who actually ship, not just talk

## Who is this for?

> [!TIP]
> If you care about **privacy**, **control**, and **budget** you're in the right room.

**Audience**

- LLM- und Agentic-Engineers
- AI-Platform-Engineers
- Hands-on Tech-Leaders

## What do you get out of it?

| You gain                        | Because we focus on                                       |
| :------------------------------ | :-------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Control & privacy**           | Local-first, on-prem, EU-cloud, trusted execution         |
| **Lower & predictable cost**    | Token economics, model selection, right-sized hardware    |
| **A real professional network** | Peers who run real workloads, not vendors selling slides  |
| **Hands-on know-how**           | Talks by practitioners, lightning sessions, open Q&A      |
| **A signal in the noise**       | Curated topics — no hype cycles, no buzzword bingo        |

## How does it work?

- **Format** — focus talks + lightning sessions + open discussion + networking
- **Cadence** — every 1–2 months
- **Location** — Vienna, in-person only (no Teams, no Zoom)
- **Language** — talks in English or German, audience decides

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **In-person only.** The value is in the hallway conversations, the whiteboard sketches, and the people you meet. We don't stream.

## Who is behind it?

A group of engineers and architects from the Vienna AI scene — builders who care more about working systems than vendor decks.

- Hosted by practitioners, for practitioners
- Independent — no vendor lock-in, no sales pitches
